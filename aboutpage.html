Swooze is a streetwear clothing brand founded by Dante in Botswana. The brand was created to introduce online and international fashion trends to Botswana.

The name Swooze comes from a mix of cozy and swag, representing comfortable clothing with confident street style. The brand focuses mainly on youth who want modern fashion and a bold streetwear look.
